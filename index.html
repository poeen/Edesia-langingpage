FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why use Edesia?

We know the struggles of managing the many aspects of running a successful food service business. We have created an app to streamline and elevate the catering experience for vendors and customers. Edesia provides an all in one app experience where you can find local gigs, submit a bid for catering, get it scheduled and recieve secure payouts!

How do I get paid?

In order to start receiving payments you will need to set up a Stripe account. Once you set up your Stripe account you are ready to start accepting same day payments.

What do I need to get started?

Request a demo, and one of our experts will reach out to you to provide next steps and answer any questions.

How do I manage my menu?

Edesia’s Grill app allows you to input your menu and photos of your delicious food on the go. You can update and change your menu at any time.

Is there a binding contract?

There are terms and conditions and a catering service agreement that are required once you sign up on the app.

How do customers find my business?

Once you onboard your catering business, you will have a section on the Edesia Gigs App that allows you to upload your menu and show what you offer. There is also a category section that allows filtering.
