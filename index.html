About Edesia

Edesia (EH-DES-EE-UH) is a mobile ordering app designed to make your food truck business easier and more efficient. Utilizing the Edesia app, customers can find your location, place orders in advance, and check in to see when their order is ready.

Mobile Ordering

Streamline Communications

As a food truck owner, you have to be in two places at once. Edesia solves this problem by working in the background of your business, making communication easy with customers. Our app streamlines communication by allowing customers to order and pay through our app, and in return, allowing you to notify customers when their order is ready.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Food truck sign up on Edesia is 100% free for food truck owners. There is a 3% transaction fees on Edesia.

Why use Edesia?

We know the struggles of managing the many aspects of running a successful food truck. We have created an app to streamline and elevate the food truck experience for food truck owners and food truck goers. Edesia provides an all in one app experience where your customers are able to locate your truck, order from your truck, and review your truck.

How do I get paid?

In order to start receiving payments you will need to set up a Stripe account. Once you set up your Stripe account you are ready to start accepting same day payments.

What do I need to get started?

Request a demo, and one of our experts will reach out to you to provide next steps and answer any questions.

How do I manage my menu?

Edesia’s Grill app allows you to input your menu and photos of your delicious food on the go. You can update and change your menu at any time.

Is there a binding contract?

There are terms and conditions amd a food truck service agreement that are required once you sign up on the app.

How do customers find my truck?

Once you onboard your truck onto the app you will have a section on the Edesia Grill that allows you to connect your calendar and post your schedule. Once customers open the app they will be able to find your truck on the map based on the locations you posted.
